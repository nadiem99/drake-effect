The Drake
Effect

A feature from Drake has, for more than a decade, functioned as a kind of cultural gateway — launching regional artists into the mainstream and pulling entire genres into North American ears. This chart shows the percentage change in cultural attention (Google search interest) around each artist's breakthrough Drake collaboration, with the collab month set to zero. Each line's peak lift and today's Spotify monthly listeners appear at the end of the line.
